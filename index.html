Terms of Service

Our Story & Service Commitment

We believe every moment deserves a melody. ForeverTunes was born from the idea that love, joy, and memories sound best when turned into song. Our journey started with a simple vision: make gifting more meaningful and deeply personal through music. By using our service, you agree to engage in a creative process with our artists to bring your stories to life through music.

Today, ForeverTunes helps you turn moments and feelings into soundtracks for those you love. Our every song is inspired by your memories, emotions, and stories, crafted to last forever. We commit to delivering a professionally crafted song based on the details you provide in your order.

Privacy Policy

Our Vision & Promise on Your Data

Our vision is to make music the most heartfelt way to express emotions. Each ForeverTune is crafted with love, creativity, and professional artistry. This extends to how we handle your personal information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that the personal stories and details you share with us are used solely for the purpose of creating your custom song.

Who We Are & How We Use Information

Our creators are musicians, storytellers, and dreamers dedicated to translating your feelings into music. Every ForeverTunes song is hand-made by people who care deeply about the craft and your story. The team, including Katie (Lead Songwriter), Sam (Composer), and Priya (Producer), will have access to the information you provide to fulfill your order. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. All information is treated with confidentiality and respect.
